Bitcoin (BTC)
Category: Store of Value / Digital Gold

Bitcoin is the original decentralized cryptocurrency, launched in 2009. It functions as digital money on a peer-to-peer blockchain network with a fixed supply of 21 million coins, serving as a hedge against inflation and enabling secure, borderless transfers without intermediaries.

Ethereum (ETH)
Category: Smart Contract Platform / Layer-1 Blockchain

Ethereum is a programmable blockchain platform powering smart contracts, decentralized applications (dApps), DeFi, and NFTs. Ether (ETH) fuels network transactions and computation, making it the backbone of Web3 innovation since its 2015 launch.

Category: Stablecoin / Fiat-Backed

Tether is the leading dollar-pegged stablecoin, providing price stability in crypto markets. It facilitates trading, remittances, and hedging with 1:1 USD backing and multi-chain availability since 2014.
